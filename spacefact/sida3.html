Black Holes

A black hole is a region of spacetime where gravity is so strong that nothing can escape from it. Not even light.

The theory of general relativity predicts that a sufficently conpact mass can deform spacetime to form a black hole.

The boundary of the region from witch no escape is possile is called the event horizon.
